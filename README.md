# Journeys into the Bright World — EPUB

An EPUB edition of *Journeys into the Bright World* (1978) by **Marcia Moore** and **Howard Sunny Alltounian, M.D.**

## About the Book

*Journeys into the Bright World* is a fascinating and deeply personal account of Marcia Moore and Dr. Howard Sunny Alltounian's pioneering exploration of ketamine as a tool for consciousness expansion and psychospiritual therapy. Part memoir, part research journal, the book documents their experiences, insights, and vision for what they called "samadhi therapy."

## How This Was Made

This EPUB was created from a full set of high-resolution page scans of the original 1978 first edition, published by Para Research, Inc. The scans were processed through OCR, then carefully formatted and corrected by hand — restoring chapter structure, typography, italics, and the original butterfly illustrations that appear at each chapter opening. The result is a clean, reflowable e-book faithful to the printed original.

## Download

The EPUB file is in the [`ebook/`](ebook/) folder. It can be opened with Apple Books, Calibre, Kobo, or any other EPUB-compatible reader.

## Why

I hope this brings this great book to more people to read and enjoy it like I did. It has been out of print for decades and is nearly impossible to find. Everyone deserves access to it.
